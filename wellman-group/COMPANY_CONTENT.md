## 6. MODULAR OPERATION THEATRE (MOT)

### Concept
A jointless and seamless structure designed and installed to provide a clean and hygienic (0.3 micron) environment inside the O.T. to avoid any kind of infection to the patient during surgery, controlling temperature, pressure and humidity. It employs control over the particulate matter in air with temperature, humidity and pressure control, thereby reducing bacteria by a factor of 15,000.

### Divisions of O.T.
- **Superspeciality O.T.** – Neuroscience, Orthopedics, Cardiothoracic & Transplant surgery
- **General O.T.** – Laparoscopy, Ophthalmology, Gynecology, ENT, Dental, etc.

### Standards
- As per NABH & JCI Standards
- Class 100/ISO5 in both green & brown field facility

### Clean Room Classification
| USA Class | ISO Equivalent | Use Case |
|-----------|---------------|----------|
| 100 | ISO 5 | Superspeciality OT |
| 1,000 | ISO 6 | General OT |
| 10,000 | ISO 7 | ICU / NICU |
| 100,000 | ISO 8 | General |

### NABH Specifications
| Feature | Superspeciality O.T. | General O.T. |
|---------|---------------------|-------------|
| Temperature | 21 ± 3°C | 21 ± 3°C |
| Pressure (Pascal) | 10–15 | 10–15 |
| Humidity (Rh) | 40–60% | 40–60% |
| Air Filtration | 10µ Pre + 5µ Fine + 0.3µ HEPA | Same |
| Plenum Box Size | 8×6 ft | 6×4 ft |
| Class US/ISO | 100/ISO 5 | 1000/ISO 6 |
| Air changes/hour | > 25 | > 25 |
| Fresh Air Component | > 5 | > 5 |
| Air Velocity (FPM) | 90–120 | 90–120 |

### NABH Guidelines
- Dedicated A.H.U. for every OT
- No Window & Split AC
- Wall, Ceiling & Flooring material: non-porous, jointless-seamless, no 90° corners, washable with anti-bacterial and anti-fungal properties
- Cleaning of pre-filters
- Validation required

### MOT Components

**Primary Component — Laminar Air Flow (LAF)**
- Vertical Laminar Air Flow kills any bacteria inside the O.T. area giving 0.3 micron clean air
- Components: Air Handling Unit (AHU), AC connected to AHU, HEPA Filters, Plenum (Filter Housing) Box above OT Table, Ducting

**Secondary Component — Jointless & Seamless Structure**
- Complete jointless and seamless structure without any 90° corners for prevention of bacterial formation in corners, joints or cracks

### Copper Piping
- **Make:** Mexflow
- **Certification:** BS EN 13348:2008 Certified, Lloyd Certified
- **Grade:** Medical Grade; Carbon Free (Copper Pipe & Elbow)

- Pressure bearing capacity: 170–180 bar
- Enhances life of NRV (Non-Return Valve)
